# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-01*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** 2Code builds high-converting landing pages, e-commerce stores, custom software, and mobile apps.
**What it does:** We design and develop digital products that convert — from marketing sites to full-stack platforms and mobile applications. Every project is built for performance and measurable results.
**Product category:** Digital services / software development
**Product type:** Service (project-based)
**Business model:** Project-based with fixed pricing per phase, plus optional maintenance packages

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** SMBs and mid-market companies across the Middle East
**Decision-makers:** Founders, CEOs, product owners, CTOs
**Primary use case:** Need a digital product built that actually drives business results
**Jobs to be done:**
- Turn visitors into customers (conversion-focused landing pages)
- Sell products online (e-commerce stores)
- Build internal tools or customer-facing platforms (custom software)
- Reach users on mobile (native and cross-platform apps)
**Use cases:**
- Startup needs a high-converting landing page for launch
- E-commerce brand needs a store that converts browsers into buyers
- Company needs custom internal tooling or a customer-facing platform
- Business wants a mobile app for iOS and Android

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Businesses invest in digital products that look good but don't convert or perform
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Templates don't convert — they're generic and untested
- Freelancers lack the full-stack capability for complex projects
- Big agencies are slow, expensive, and don't care about results
**What it costs them:** Lost revenue from poor conversion rates, wasted development budgets, delayed time-to-market
**Emotional tension:** Fear of investing heavily in something that looks impressive but doesn't move the needle

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other digital agencies in MENA region — often lack conversion focus or technical depth
**Secondary:** Freelance developers/designers — cheaper but fragmented, no end-to-end ownership
**Indirect:** DIY platforms (Shopify themes, Webflow, no-code tools) — fast but generic, limited customization

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Conversion-first approach ("built to perform, not just impress")
- Technology-agnostic — right tool for the job, not one-size-fits-all
- End-to-end ownership from discovery through post-launch optimization
- Design craft with engineering depth
**How we do it differently:** Every element is tested, every interaction is intentional. We don't ship pretty screens — we ship results.
**Why that's better:** Clients see measurable lifts in conversion, engagement, and revenue
**Why customers choose us:** Attention to craft, conversion results, seamless team integration
