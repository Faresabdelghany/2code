1. Executive Summary

Good news: your single-page site already has strong fundamentals — clean meta tags, three types of structured data, and polished brand copy. Single-page sites can absolutely rank well when optimized correctly: they concentrate all backlink equity on one URL and typically load 30% faster than multi-page sites.

What needs work: the copy is beautifully written but leans heavily on brand voice at the expense of keywords. Several statistical claims lack substantiation (a compliance risk). And there are quick technical wins — font optimization, image lazy loading, preloading — that will improve Core Web Vitals without touching the design.

This report covers only changes you can make to your existing page. No new pages, no blog, no multi-page rebuild.

2. Brand Voice & Copy Review

Overall Assessment

Biggest strength: The copy has a confident, premium feel that positions 2Code above template-shop competitors. Phrases like "We design interfaces that feel inevitable" and "Every element tested, every interaction intentional" convey craft and intentionality.

Biggest opportunity: The voice is consistent but could work harder for SEO. Nearly every heading prioritizes style over searchability. You can keep the brand tone while weaving in keywords — see the before/after rewrites below.

3. Legal & Compliance Flags

Several claims on the page could be challenged if not backed by evidence. This matters for both Google's E-E-A-T guidelines and potential legal exposure.

ClaimIssueSeverityRecommended Action
"3x Avg. Conversion Lift"Unsubstantiated superlative. What's the baseline? Across how many projects? What time period? Without context, this could be seen as misleading.HighQualify it: "3x average conversion lift across 15+ landing page projects (2023–2025)" or link to a case study. If you can't substantiate it, soften to "up to 3x".
"40% sales increase for Harvest & Co"Client-specific claim without attribution. Is this approved by the client? Time period? Compared to what baseline?HighAdd context: "40% sales increase within 6 months of launch" and ensure you have written permission from Harvest & Co to use their name and data.
"98% client satisfaction on custom builds"Measurement undefined. How was this measured? Survey? NPS? Self-reported? Without methodology, this is an unsubstantiated claim.HighAdd a qualifier: "Based on post-project client surveys" or replace with a more verifiable metric like "50+ projects delivered with repeat clients".
"50+ Projects Delivered"Relatively safe but should be kept current as the number grows.LowKeep updated. Consider showing a live counter or date stamp ("as of 2026").
Sarah Mitchell testimonial — no disclosureIf FinanceFlow is a current client or received compensation, FTC guidelines require disclosure for testimonials.MediumIf she's a real client with a real quote, you're fine — but adding a link to FinanceFlow or LinkedIn strengthens credibility.
"12+ industries served"Vague. Which industries? This weakens rather than strengthens credibility without specifics.LowReplace with: "Serving fintech, e-commerce, real estate, healthcare, and more" — naming industries adds keyword value AND credibility.

B. Technical Improvements (Impact: Medium-High)

#ActionDetailsEffort
1Deploy to 2code.agencyYour canonical tag already points to 2code.agency, but the site lives on vercel.app. This mismatch confuses Google. Set up the custom domain in Vercel and 301-redirect the vercel.app subdomain.1 hour
2Remove dead footer linksBlog, About, Careers links point to pages that don't exist. Replace with anchor links like #services, #process, #contact or remove entirely.15 min
3Add loading="lazy" to all images below the foldImproves Largest Contentful Paint (LCP). Keep the hero/above-fold images eager-loaded.15 min
4Preload the primary fontAdd <link rel="preload" href="/font.woff2" as="font" crossorigin> for DM Sans (your body font) to prevent Flash of Unstyled Text.10 min
5Reduce from 3 font families to 2You load Playfair Display, DM Sans, and Cormorant Garamond. Pick 2. Each font family adds ~50-100KB and a render-blocking request.1-2 hours
6Convert logo from PNG to SVGSVGs are resolution-independent, typically smaller, and render crisper on all screens.30 min
7Add <main> landmarkWrap your page content in <main> for accessibility and semantic HTML. Google uses landmarks for content understanding.5 min
8Add descriptive alt text to all imagesExample: alt="E-commerce website mockup for Harvest & Co showing product catalog and checkout flow"30 min
9Expand the XML sitemapEven for a single page, include lastmod, and add any anchor section URLs if you use #hash fragments for navigation (though Google doesn't index fragments, the sitemap validates your canonical).10 min
10Submit to Google Search ConsoleVerify the custom domain, submit your sitemap, and request indexing. This is step zero for tracking organic performance.30 min
