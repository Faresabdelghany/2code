1. Executive Summary

2Code has a solid on-page SEO foundation — the homepage features well-crafted meta tags, structured data (Organization, LocalBusiness, FAQPage), and a clear keyword focus on software development services in Egypt. However, the site's single-page architecture is the biggest bottleneck: all content lives on one URL, which severely limits keyword targeting, internal linking opportunities, and crawlable surface area.

Top 3 priorities that will have the most impact:

  1. Expand to a multi-page site — create dedicated service pages, case studies, and a blog to unlock keyword rankings across dozens of terms.
  2. Launch a content strategy — competitors like Enozom and LoadServ are winning with blogs, case studies, and technology pages. 2Code has none.
  3. Fix technical gaps — add a comprehensive sitemap, optimize Core Web Vitals (font loading, JS bundles), and add image optimization.

Overall assessment: Strong brand foundation, but needs structural expansion to compete for organic traffic.
